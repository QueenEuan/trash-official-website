### Short Bio

TRASH is a Taiwanese rock band founded in Taipei in December 2009. Signed with Warner Music Taiwan, the band blends Taiwanese rock, Mandopop rock, Alternative Rock, Britpop, and Grunge into a catalog shaped by emotional directness, live momentum, and arena-scale ambition.

### Long Bio

TRASH formed in Taipei in December 2009 and grew from the independent rock circuit into a major contemporary Taiwanese rock act. Early recognition followed the 2012 first album and Gongliao Ocean Music Festival phase. Later releases including `歸零`, `11:11`, `Never Die`, `Holy Trip!`, and `幸福的末班車 / The Last Train of Happiness` built a larger archive of songs, visuals, and live milestones.

In 2024 and 2025, TRASH’s 15th-anniversary era expanded into Taipei Arena and Kaohsiung Arena concerts. In 2025, the band won Best Band at the Golden Melody Awards.
